HERE IS SOME PLACES IN EGYPT TO VISIT

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient wonders near Cairo. The Egyptian Museum in Cairo has treasures like King Tut’s mask. Luxor and the Valley of the Kings have tombs and temples. The Abu Simbel Temples are huge rock temples in Aswan. You can take a cruise on the Nile River. The Red Sea resorts like Sharm El Sheikh and Hurghada offer beaches and diving. The Citadel of Saladin in Cairo has great views. Alexandria is a city with ancient ruins and a modern library. The Siwa Oasis is a peaceful desert area with temples and hot springs. Karnak Temple in Luxor is an impressive ancient site. Khan El Khalili Bazaar in Cairo is a busy market, perfect for buying traditional crafts, spices, and souvenirs. It’s a great place to experience local culture and the hustle of Cairo.These are some of the best places to visit in Egypt!
